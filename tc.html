In a nutshell

We might charge you the folowing fees:

  • an annual fee for issuing and renewing the card;
  • dihonor fees, if a cheque or payment order is dishonoured;
  • administrative fees for replacing a card or providing, at your request, copies of records, statements, etc;
  • an overlimit fee if the current balance exceeds your credit limit;
  • booking fees, if an airline or hotel reservation is cancelled or not used and the organisation sets a rate to be charged still;
  • other fees for services or facilities provided by us for anything we do in connection with this agreement, for example, fees to our collection agents if you are in default.

Note: We decide the amount of fees and charges and the calculation method. We might vary these and we might not be able to give you notice.
